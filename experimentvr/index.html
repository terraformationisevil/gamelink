Grip = grab / throw cubes, balls, or weapons (swap hands by grabbing with the other one)
Trigger = aim & teleport (cooldown adjustable on the settings board)  |  Left stick = move  |  Right stick = turn
Press Y x3 in 2s = spawn cube in hand  |  Press X x3 in 2s = spawn ball in hand  |  X/A = shoot when holding a gun
Craft table: cycle recipes (pistol/rifle/sniper/sword/pickaxe/numbers 1-9), then shoot the materials to craft  |  Mine rocks with the pickaxe  |  Settings board: toggle spawning, waves, teleport, and its cooldown
