Grip = grab / throw cubes, balls, or weapons (swap hands by grabbing with the other one)
Trigger = shoot when holding a gun  |  X/A = aim & teleport (cooldown adjustable on the settings board)  |  Left stick = move  |  Right stick = turn  |  Left stick click = jump
Press Y x3 in 2s = spawn cube in hand  |  Press left trigger x3 in 2s (empty-handed) = spawn ball in hand
Craft table: cycle recipes, then shoot the materials to craft  |  Mine rocks with the pickaxe (1.5x damage)  |  All weapons have infinite ammo  |  Settings board: toggle spawning, waves, teleport, and its cooldown
