Card Inventory Replaces modifier tags with a Cards button. Your chosen modifiers are stored in a card inventory showing how many times you've picked each.
Ability Inventory DEP: CARD INVENTORY Enables the Items category in Card Inventory. Abilities are stored there. You can delete items and abilities in the Items tab.
Item Tiles DEP: ABILITY INVENTORY 5% chance for a tile to give a random item. Common (60%), Rare (30%), Absurd (10%). Scrap always gives 1 item.
Modifier Crafting DEP: ITEM TILES Adds a Crafting tab to the inventory. Craft items and modifiers using your collected items.
Exchange DEP: CARD INVENTORY Adds an Exchange tab. Trade 2 common cards for a random common card (costs 10% of credits).
Inheritance DEP: ITEM TILES Enables the Inheritance one-round modifier. Requires losing a run with "The Will" in your inventory to unlock.
